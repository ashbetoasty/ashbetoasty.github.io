Wrestling

There are many different kinds of "wrestling". Professional Wrestling, Lucha Libre Wrestling, roughing around. However, real wrestling is Olympic Style Wrestling. Most people would consider Wrestling as the hardest spor out there. Sure, other sports are pretty hard to do, and require a certain skil that you might not have. But, Wrestling is something that everyone could do, just not something that every wants to. The objective of wrestling is to win, but there are a few different ways to achieve that win. As always, you could pin your opponent, which means putting them on their back around their shoulders for a 3-count. But if that's unattainable, you could always win by points. Each Wrestling match consits of 3 periods, each period varying in length from 1 minute to 3 minutes. To win by points, you just have to finish a match with more points scored that your opponent. However, there is another way to win by points... tech fall. A tech fall is when you score ennough points that you have a lead on you opponent by at least 15 points. Of course, you could always win if your opponent gets an injury during the match, but that's not a real.
