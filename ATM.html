Reflection

Bancroft's ATM has taught me so much about computers. I've learned how to type corectly to be a more effecient typers. I've even learned how to properly use the Adobe programs! ATM has put me above my peers, because it helps me do better, faster computer work, and it allows me to turn in more advanced looking papers for assignments. The program itself is truly amazing, it takes kids who are already good at, or interested in, computers, and it lets them take their skills to the next level.
